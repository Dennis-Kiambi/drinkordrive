Cocktails

Frozen Strawberry Margarita

Ingredients

  • Tequila
  • Strawberry margarita mix
  • Triple sec
  • One cup frozen strawberries
  • Ice
  • Garnish - lemon wheel

Directions

  • Add ice, frozen strawberries, tequila, triple sec
    and strawberry margarita mix to blender.
  • Combine until smooth.
  • Pour mix into glass and garnish with a lime wheel.


Alien Secretion

Ingredients

  • Coconut Rum
  • Pineapple juice
  • Melon liqueur
  • Ice
  • Garnish - Cherry

Directions

  • Rim the edge of your glass using blue sugar. Set aside.
  • In a shaking glass with ice, combine coconut rum,
    melon liqueur, and pineapple juice.
  • Shake well.
  • Strain mix into rimmed glass over ice and garnish with a cherry.


Dutch Mojito

Ingredients

  • 30ml Simple syrup
  • 45ml Gin
  • 3 Lime wedges
  • Bar spoon brown sugar
  • 8 Mint leaves
  • Sparklling water
  • Ice
  • Garnish - Mint sprouts

Directions

  • To base of glass add lime wedges, mint leaves and brown sugar.
  • Add ice to glass and pour over jenever gin and simple syrup.
  • Stir lightly.
  • Top with sparkling water and garnish with a mint sprig.
